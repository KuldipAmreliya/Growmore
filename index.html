courses we are expert in

We use only the finest quality materials and cutting-edge technology in all our jewelry design courses, ensuring that each creation reflects exceptional craftsmanship and elegance.

  • CAD (Computer-Aided Design)

    "Master the art of jewelry design with our CAD course. Learn to create precise digital models and bring your unique ideas to life using industry-leading software.

  • Rhino Jewellery Design

    Jewellery design software like Rhino offers precise 3D modeling tools tailored for creating intricate Jewellery designs. Its benefits include faster prototyping, accurate detailing, and easy modifications, enhancing efficiency and creativity in the Jewellery design process.

  • Matrix Jewellery Design

    ewellery Matrix design software streamlines the creation of complex Jewellery designs, providing specialized tools for gemstone placement and design customization. Its benefits include improved design accuracy, reduced production time, and enhanced creativity, making it an invaluable tool for Jewellery designers.

  • Jewellery Manual Design

    Manual Jewellery designing by hand allows for a personal touch and unique craftsmanship. Its benefits include the ability to create one-of-a-kind pieces, a deep connection to the artistic process, and the potential for intricate, artisanal designs.
