Our Story

Our passion and purpose

At Grow More Jewelry Design Institute, we are dedicated to empowering aspiring jewelry designers with the skills and knowledge to bring their artistic visions to life.

Born from a deep appreciation for the artistry of jewelry making, our founders recognized the need for a modern, innovative institute in the heart of Surat. With this vision, we created a space where creativity meets craftsmanship, blending contemporary techniques with timeless design principles.

Trained and experienced in leading global design hubs like Paris, Milan, and New York City, our team brings world-class expertise to every aspect of our curriculum. Grow More Jewelry Design Institute embodies a fusion of global inspiration and local artistry, preparing designers to shine in the competitive world of jewelry design
